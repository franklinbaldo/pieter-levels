## Key decisions
- Focus purely on simple, low-friction monetization of existing assets (data, content, APIs, SaaS wrappers).
- Avoid building complex billing systems upfront; use landing pages and simple payment links to validate willingness to pay.

## Lessons learned
- Franklin's projects are primarily open-source, exploratory, and civic-focused. There is huge untapped potential to package these as simple B2B tools or APIs.
- See `memory/notes/monetization-strategy.md` for the core Pieter Levels approach applied to Franklin's stack.
